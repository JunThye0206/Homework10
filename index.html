Mining in Australia

This visualisation is about mining in Australia.

Australian Mines

There are mines located in all states of Australia with Western Australia, Queensland and New South Wales having the most mines. Both Queensland and New South Wales focus on metal deposits and also coal. Whereas Western Australia focusses on metal deposits and iron.

Mining Expenditure in Australia

For the most years the actual mining exploration expenses exceeded the expected mining exploration expenses. The biggest difference was in 2021 where the amount exceeded was up to 14% (442.9 Million). However this is not true for the year 2020, where the actual expense was lesser than the expected expense. This is due to the pandemic that happened in 2020 which made the mining industry stop working during that time.
